About Project

For the final project, my group decided to explore COVID-19 vaccination rates across US counties, allowing users to understand geographic disparities in vaccine uptake through an explorable map interface. By combining county-level CDC vaccination data with interactive D3.js visualizations, the project enables both broad national trends and granular local analysis. As of now our D3 visualization is not complete, however, at completion it will depict a map of the United States with counties colored based on their vaccination rates, and we are currently exploring the idea of including other statistics such as education levels and GDP contribution alongside vaccination rate, however, this is just a consideration for the time being.
